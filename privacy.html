Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

At EVOQ (Elite Visibility & Outreach Quotient), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle the information you provide when requesting a consultation for our sales training services in Kenya.

2. Information We Collect

When you engage with our platform or request a consultation, we collect the following personal data:

  • Contact Data: Name, business email address, and phone number.
  • Professional Data: Job title, company name, and industry (if provided).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data via cookies to improve your experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

EVOQ utilizes your information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate the requested Sales Strategy Consultation.
  • To verify your organization's suitability for our elite training frameworks.
  • To send relevant communication regarding your inquiry via Zapier-integrated email systems.
  • To improve our outreach quotient and website performance.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. However, your data is processed through secure third-party service providers to ensure a seamless experience:

  • Zapier: For automation of lead management.
  • Gmail/Workspace: For secure communication.

5. Your Rights (Kenya Data Protection Act)

In accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019), you have the following rights:

  • The right to be informed of how your data is used.
  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request the deletion of your data from our systems.
  • The right to object to marketing communications.

6. Security

We implement professional-grade security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Access to your data is strictly limited to the EVOQ Executive Team.
